Salman Kizhissseri's New Single 'Thalassery Vibe' Launched!

Sarigama Music Corner is proud to announce the official launch of **'Thalassery Vibe'**, the much-anticipated single by our versatile artist, **Salman Kizhissseri**. This track marks a new direction for Salman, blending his signature soulful vocals with rich, electronic soundscapes inspired by the North Malabar coastal rhythms.

The single captures the energetic and unique cultural feel of Thalassery, making it a perfect anthem for the modern Kerala youth while staying true to our musical roots.

The Production Story

The entire track was meticulously recorded, mixed, and mastered at the international-standard Sarigama Music Corner studio in Kerala. Lead producer **Rafi Poovattuparamba** spearheaded the mixing process, ensuring that the traditional instruments like the Udukkai and Chenda were perfectly balanced with the modern synth and basslines. This collaborative effort took over three months, guaranteeing a final sound that is both authentic and globally competitive.

Where to Listen

The single is now available on all major streaming platforms including Spotify, YouTube Music, and Apple Music. Be sure to check out the official music video for a full cinematic experience!
